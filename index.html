City of Berthold

Berthold is located in Ward County, North Dakota. Home of the Berthold Bombers. You can find an abundance of hunting opportunities including pheasant, duck, geese and fishing within an hour or less from town. Lake Darling Dam, Tioga Dam, and Lake Sakakawea all offer plenty of fishing areas and ice-fishing in the winter. Many campsites are also available; please inquire if you have any questions.
